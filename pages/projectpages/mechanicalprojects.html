2002 Subaru Impreza WRX Autocross/Daily Project

Summer 2023 - Today

General/Motivation:

This ongoing project aims to setup my personal vehicle for the dual purpose of daily-driving and autocrossing. Building a vehicle for these two tasks requires walking a fine line between performance and comfort, particularly in the areas of suspension tuning, drivetrain setup, and damping.

Sub-Projects To Date:

  • Rear differential complete DIY rebuild with a KAAZ 1.5 way clutch-type LSD
  • Complete bushing/mount replacement with ployurethane/stiffened-rubber bushings
  • Full suspension rebuild with tuned geometry for mixed street/autocross use
  • Re-application of rear-body seam sealer joints to prevent water leakage
  • Complete floor/underbody rust repair/prevention
  • Suspension corner balancing via corner weight analysis
  • Front light rewire with load resistors to convert from halogen to LED bulbs

Abandoned 2000 Celica GTS Revival Project

Spring 2023 - Winter 2024

General/Motivation:

This completed project aimed to revive a $500 Celica that had been sitting non-running in a driveway for about half a decade. Once the project was complete, I found it a new home.

Sub-Projects To Date:

  • Electrical system diagnostics to regain power to the starter motor
  • Interior deep clean to deal with years worth of mold
  • Driver-side floorpan cut-and-weld replacement to deal with advanced rust that had destroyed the seat mounting points underneath the carpet
  • Freeing jammed sunroof mechanism and clearing non-functional sunroof drain pipes that likely caused the advanced rust
  • General motor refresh with upgraded "lift-bolts" for the VVTI-L timing system, and new gaskets

Abandoned 1984 Volvo 240 Revival Project
