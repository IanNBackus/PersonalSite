Immersive Animal Anatomy Project

OSU Senior Capstone

Ian Backus, Quinn Hoffman, Raffaele De Amicis

General/Motivation:

Textbook diagrams are insufficient for deliberate studying, and modern anatomical models are expensive and delicate! Cadavers are the best way to study anatomy but can be quite impractical to source on a regular basis, and arenâ€™t suited towards a large lecture environment. To help Veterinary students learn anatomy we have created a program that allows for immersive, collaborative studying in a VR environment.

Features:

  • Dissect realistic anatomical models
  • Use learning tools including labeling, note-taking, quizzes, and more
  • Interact with a VR environment
  • Invite classmates/friends/instructors to work with you in multiplayer lobbies
  • Study anywhere with the Meta Quest 2 standalone mode
  • All functional through your Oculus/META account
  • Available for download on the Meta App Lab once you're whitelisted
