General:

Mecop is a program that works to connect students, universities, and employers in a way where all benefit from the collaboration. Students receive the opportunity to participate in two six-month internships to both build their knowledge and establish work-experience/connections, employers get the chance to employ new and eager talent onto innovative projects, and universities benefit from a better connected student-base who can apply their learnings back into their work at school.
